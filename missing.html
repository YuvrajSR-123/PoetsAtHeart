THOSE DAYS ARE MISSING

Just going through the old conversation, I finally found the old we
When we used to talk for hours insanely and carefree
When for your one look, I could walk a mile
And on seeing my stupidity, you gave a cute love smile
Those smiles are lost somewhere, that madness is missing now
We are not old we, we have changed - but why and how

When my every profile change comes with a lot of praises from you
When my every new look was a matter of laughter at times for you
Those good morning messages are lost somewhere
Those happy moments are lost - now they seemed to be rare

When the journey used to be more beautiful than destination
When there used to be dark side of the vacations
When a day without you seemed to be the day wasted
When you easily recognize the fake smile on my face pasted
Those cute conversations are lost, those healthy flirtings are missing somewhere
Lost are the days when we used to give each other every impossible dare
Lost are the days when love was in the air
