CAN YOU STAY A LITTLE LONGER

With all the emotions and the promises made , I trusted you
I gave you that place in my heart which was known by few
I thought your presence will change my life , as friendship will grew
But my life will change this much , I had no clue
                                       All those promises made were false, all those feelings were fake
                                       I thought those friendship and love bonds will never break
                                       'Me without you' was never thought , never excepted
                                       We sweared - me and you will always be "we" , we will always remain connected
Some hard situation and you left with no convincing reason , you were afraid
Instead of facing them , you backed off , you forgot all the moments we made
Please don't leave , I dont have anyone just you
My one mistake can affect you this much , I never knew
I promise , from now you will never be hurted again
Please give me a chance , You dont know my condition right now , I am in much pain
                                       I am missing my gossiping partner , those late night absurd talks are missed badly
                                       You dont know how much my heart is searching yours madly
                                       You and I can be We again , I can promise you this coz I believe
                                       Just stay with me , give me one chance , please dont leave
